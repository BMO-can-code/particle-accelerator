Physics Reference
Synchrotron Principle:
R = p / (qB) — fixed orbit radius, B increases with momentum

RF Acceleration:
ΔE = q × V_rf — energy gain per revolution

Cyclotron Frequency:
f = qB / (2πγm) — decreases as γ increases

Relativistic Limit:
E² = (pc)² + (mc²)² — total energy
KE = (γ − 1)mc² — kinetic energy

Synchrotron Radiation:
P = (c₂/2π) × E⁴/(ρ²m⁴) — power radiated
Dominant for electrons, negligible for protons
