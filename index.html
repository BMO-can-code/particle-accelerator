🧲 Magnetic Bending

A uniform B-field creates the Lorentz force F = q(v × B), bending the proton into a circular orbit.

📡 RF Cavity

Each revolution through the cavity adds ΔKE = qV of energy — the same principle behind CERN's LHC.

⚡ Relativity

As v → c, the Lorentz factor γ grows nonlinearly, making acceleration progressively harder.
